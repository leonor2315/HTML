Legendary Musicians and Their Instruments

Here are some of the most famous musicians in history and the instruments they are known for:

Jimi Hendrix

Instrument: Electric Guitar

Jimi Hendrix revolutionized the sound of the electric guitar and is considered one of the greatest guitarists of all time.

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

Instrument: Piano

Mozart was a prolific composer and virtuoso pianist, known for his timeless classical compositions.

Yo-Yo Ma

Instrument: Cello

Yo-Yo Ma is one of the most celebrated cellists in the world, known for his mastery of the instrument and cross-genre collaborations.

Beyoncé

Instrument: Voice

Beyoncé is a global icon, known for her powerful voice and dynamic performances.
