Avocado Toast Deluxe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Lemon juice, salt, pepper
  • Chili flakes
  • Optional: poached egg, cherry tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Toast bread to your liking.
  2. Mash avocado with lemon, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread on toast; top with chili flakes.
  4. Add poached egg or tomatoes if desired.
