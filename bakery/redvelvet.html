Red Velvet Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • Flour, sugar, cocoa
  • Eggs
  • Buttermilk
  • Oil
  • Red food coloring
  • Vinegar, baking soda
  • Cream cheese frosting

Instructions

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately; combine.
  2. Fill cupcake liners 2/3 full.
  3. Bake at 175°C (350°F) for 18–20 min.
  4. Cool and frost with cream cheese icing.
