Vegan Buddha Bowl

Ingredients

  • Cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • Roasted sweet potatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Avocado, spinach
  • Tahini dressing

Instructions

  1. Arrange cooked grains in a bowl.
  2. Add roasted veggies, chickpeas, and greens.
  3. Drizzle with tahini dressing.
  4. Serve fresh with lemon wedges.
