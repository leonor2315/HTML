Our Coffee Selection

Ethiopian Yirgacheffe

Tasting Notes: Bright, citrus, floral.

A bright, medium-bodied coffee with distinct floral and citrus notes. Grown in the Yirgacheffe region of Ethiopia, this coffee is a favorite among connoisseurs.

Recommended Brewing: Pour-over, Drip.

Colombian Supremo

Tasting Notes: Nutty, chocolate, mild fruit.

A well-balanced, medium-bodied coffee with a nutty and chocolatey flavor profile and a clean finish. Supremo beans are the largest and highest quality from Colombia.

Recommended Brewing: French Press, Espresso.

Sumatra Mandheling

Tasting Notes: Earthy, herbal, low acidity.

A dark, full-bodied coffee with a rich, earthy, and herbal flavor. Known for its low acidity, this Indonesian coffee is a bold and intense experience.

Recommended Brewing: Drip, French Press.
